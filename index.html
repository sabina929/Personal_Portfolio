About Me

I love being able to create. I am always eager to learn new skills and passionate in what I am doing. My skills stretch over a variety of areas with focus on front-end development, web, graphic, and motion design, and digital art. So I believe that I can make projects remarkable and accessible as well by using my design and art skills. I currently have knowledge of developing web projects using HTML5, CSS3/Sass, JavaScript, React, GSAP3, jQuery and Bootstrap.

In my spare time, I like to try creating new/enjoyable things on my CodePen account, sketching or painting some artworks on Sketchbook or Photoshop, digital sculpting on Zbrush, reading science-fiction, fantasy, and comic books, medical and tech articles and playing indie video games.
